Chicken Parmigiana

Description

Chicken parmigiana, often referred to as "chicken parm" or pollo alla parmigiana, is a popular Italian-American dish consisting of breaded chicken breast topped with tomato sauce and mozzarella, parmesan, or provolone cheese.

This chicken parmigiana is a very nice dinner for two. Serve it with your favorite pasta and tossed greens.
